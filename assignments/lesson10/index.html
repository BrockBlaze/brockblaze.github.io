Southern Idaho

by Brock B.

Southern Idaho is a great place to visit and has many activities you can do. You can go fishing, hiking, camping, and play sports. Idaho is truly a gem state. Go hiking to find that perfect view and picture, Go fishing to catch the biggest fish that you have every seen in your life. Go camping to enjoy the escape from the world and that nice fresh air, and finaly play some sports to lose that weight that you told yourself that you would lose a long time ago jack.
